Product allergen lookup

Live, label-declared allergens from Open Food Facts, mapped to your preference columns.

Use this to verify a real product you buy. Best results come from the barcode (the 13-digit EAN on the package). Name search is a convenience — relevance can be loose, so always confirm you picked the right brand/size. Open Food Facts is crowdsourced and may be incomplete or out of date for a given product, so the physical product label remains the legal source of truth for allergy-critical decisions. Declared allergens follow EU Reg. 1169/2011 Annex II; the intolerance columns (Lactose, Fructose, Citrus, Stone fruit, Histamine) are not declarable allergens and will not appear here.

Data: Open Food Facts (openfoodfacts.org), retrieved live when you search. This tool sends only your query to Open Food Facts and stores nothing.
